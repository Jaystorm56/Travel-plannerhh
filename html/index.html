Delivery Solutions for Online Retailers

Quickly integrate powerful solutions that give you more flexibility and
control over your parcel shipping and logistics processes.

Nationwide Delivery

We provide logistics services where
we deliver to all customers in any
location across the country.

Payment on Delivery

We provide a service where we
collect payment on delivery in Lagos,
Abuja and Portharcourt. If the goods
are not paid for, they are returned to
the shipper.

Reverse Logistics & Returns

We provide a service where we return
all shipments to the originating
location for the shipper to pick-up if
unsuccessful. Shipper is contacted
upon return to the location.

Warehousing Support

We take care of your storage and
distribution logistics needs, utilizing
our strategic network of warehouses
across the country.
